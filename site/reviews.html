One piece

Probably the best shonen manga i've read, it's characters are so charming and lovable even for a long ass manga like this one that has a LOT of characters, obviously there's some that i don't remember their names but they're still lovable yk. The world building of this is a MASTERPIECE, it just does not stop surprising you with the mysteries that it has, i've found myself constantly shocked, even crying just because of the emotion of discovering new things, it's just so good. 9.8/10

Kaiju No. 8

This one was a lot of fun to watch, so far i've only watched the anime, so i don't think i'm nearly as far in the story of this one. If i had to remark one thing it would be the OST, it was just so fucking good, the guitar riff that sounded everytime the protag transformed was amazing, specially in THAT scene where he just fucking appeared behind the villain, i almost screamed, seriously. Basically it was pretty fun and the action was pretty good, i loved the characters too. 7/10

Blue period

This one is not finished yet, and i'm in the last chapter of the manga (currently). To be honest, if it weren't for this manga (and other pieces of media too) i wouldn't even do the effort to make this page, it just speaked to me, it made me appreciate art in a lot of ways i didn't already did. I just want to say that, if you like the humanity that resides in art, i can't recommend you this manga nearly enough. Just read it. You'll thank me. 9/10

Adventure Time

My favorite western cartoon. It starts with a refreshing type of comedy cartoons haven't seen in a lot of time, season 1 and 2 are excellent in making you laugh while introducing (little by little) elements of the background lore it has. To not spoil anything to anyone, my favorite part of this series is the process of madurity Finn goes through, specially with the 3 love interest he has (the last one is the one for Finn, believe me.) I wont talk about Distant Islands or Fionna and Cake here because while they're part of adventure time as a franchise, i'm talking about the main series. 8.5/10

Clannad

I'm currently rewatching season 1 with my fiancé, but i've already watched it like at least 6 years ago. I remember watching episode 2 and crying like a fucking bitch istg. This anime is the one that never fails to make me cry uncontrollable, like i can't talk without breaking into tears, it's horrible (in a good way). I haven't played the visual novel and i don't intend to do so. Solid 7.5/10

Clannad After Story

I won't say a lot bcs my fiancé will read this, but omfg. WHYYYYY WOULD YOU DO THIS TO MEEEE. 9/10

House M.D.

As i'm writing this i'm currently in season 1 episode 14. Why is this show so fucking entertaining? I don't love medical shows, in fact, I despise them. I hate grey's anatomy, I hate the one with the bad representation of autism, and I hate all copies of that one, they're so boring for some reason. But house... I think it's his personality, the way he is a fucking asshole with everyone but he's still the best doctor they have so they can't fire him. I know it's not the best writing in a show but goddamn it's entertaining as hell. Love watching it while i'm doing something or can't sleep 6/10
